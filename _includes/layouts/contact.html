Other disaster recovery needs

If you are interested in other disaster recovery resources outside of mutual aid for building departments,  FEMA offers a variety of tools and programs to assist in the recovery process.

Whether you need financial assistance, legal aid, or guidance on managing long-term recovery, FEMA provides comprehensive support to help you rebuild and recover. Below are some key resources and contact points to get you started:

  • DisasterAssistance.gov: Apply for individual disaster assistance and track your application status.
  • FEMA Helpline: Call 1-800-621-3362 for general questions about disaster assistance.
  • Community Recovery Management Toolkit: Access resources to help manage long-term recovery.
  • Recovery and Resilience Resource Library: Find and research federal disaster recovery resources.
